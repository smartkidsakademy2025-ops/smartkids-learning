Keeping Your Child
Safe Online.

🛡️

The internet is a vast playground of information, but like any playground, children need to know the "rules of the park" to stay safe while exploring.

1. The Golden Rule of Privacy

Teach your children that personal information is like a secret key. Full names, home addresses, school names, and phone numbers should never be shared with "online friends" without a parent's permission.

Kidventor Pro Tip

Create a "Family Tech Contract." Sit down with your child and decide together which sites are okay to visit and what times are for "screen-off" focus.

2. Think Before You Click

Pop-ups and "Free Download" buttons are often traps. At Kidventor, we teach students to identify suspicious links and understand that if something looks too good to be true online, it usually is.

3. Kindness in the Digital World

Our moral values curriculum emphasizes Digital Empathy. We teach children that behind every screen name is a real person with real feelings. Being a "Kidventor" means being a leader who stands against cyberbullying.
