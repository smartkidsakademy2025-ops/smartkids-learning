Time Management ⏳

Time is your second examiner. If you waste time on one difficult question, you may lose 5 easy marks elsewhere.

✅ Answer easy questions first
✅ Skip confusing questions quickly
✅ Return later for hard ones
✅ Leave 10 minutes for review

Why Every Child Must Read 📚

Reading improves understanding, speed, confidence, and memory. A student who reads daily performs better in every subject — English, Mathematics, Science, Government, and even CBT exams.

✅ Better understanding of questions
✅ Faster answering speed
✅ Strong vocabulary
✅ Higher confidence in exams

Effective Reading Style

1. Read daily
Even 20 minutes every day matters.
2. Remove distractions
Keep phone away while reading.
3. Use a pen
Underline important points.
4. Revise again
Revision helps memory stay longer.
