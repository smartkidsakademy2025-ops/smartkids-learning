Become a Cyber Hero

The internet is an adventure, but every hero needs a shield. Learn the rules of the road to keep yourself and your family safe.

Rule #1: The Secret

Keep Your "Key" Hidden

Your personal information is like the key to your house. You wouldn't give it to a stranger on the street, so don't give it to a stranger online!

❌ Full Name & Address
❌ Phone Numbers
❌ School Name
🤐

Privacy Meter: High

PASSWORD POWER TEST
123456 WEAK
Blue#Elephant!99 STRONG

Build a Strong Fortress

A strong password is long and uses symbols like @, #, !. Never use your birthday or your pet's name. It should be easy for you to remember, but impossible for a computer to guess!

Spot the Red Flags 🚩

🎁

Free Prizes

If a website says you won a "Free iPhone," it is likely a trap. Don't click it!

🕵️‍♂️

Stranger Chat

If someone you don't know in real life asks to meet you, tell a parent immediately.

⚠️

Scary Pop-ups

If a screen says "Your computer is infected," don't panic. Just close the tab.

What Would You Do? 🤔

📩

Strange Email

You receive an email saying: “Congratulations! Click here to win ₦500,000!”

Correct Action: Do NOT click it. Tell a parent or teacher first.
👤

Online Stranger

Someone you do not know asks: “Send me your photo and home address.”

Correct Action: Never reply. Report immediately.

Stop Cyberbullying 💜

Cyberbullying means using the internet to hurt, threaten, or embarrass someone. It can happen through messages, comments, pictures, or fake accounts.

🚫

Do Not Join

Never help people bully others online.

📢

Speak Up

Tell a trusted adult immediately.

❤️

Be Kind

Use the internet to encourage people.

The "Golden Rule" of the Web

"If you wouldn't say it or show it to someone in person, don't do it online."

Always be kind and respectful. Your digital footprint lasts forever!
