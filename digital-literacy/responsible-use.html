The Wise Digital Citizen

With great tech comes great responsibility. Every click you make and every word you type leaves a mark. Let’s make sure your mark is a great one!

Your Digital Footprint 👣

Everything you do online—comments, photos, searches, likes, and messages—is like a permanent footprint in the sand. Once it is online, it can stay there for a very long time.

“The internet never forgets. Before you post, ask: Would I want my future boss or my grandmother to see this?”

Good vs Bad Footprint

Your Online Footprints Matter 👣

Every post, comment, photo, and message leaves a mark online. Make sure your footprint tells a good story about you.

✅

Good Digital Footprint

Helping classmates online
Posting respectful comments
Sharing school projects
Learning useful skills online
❌

Bad Digital Footprint

Cyberbullying others
Sharing fake information
Posting rude comments
Uploading dangerous content

Be an “Upstander,” Not a Bystander 🛡️

If you see someone being mean online, you have the power to help.

Don’t Feed the Trolls

Mean people want a reaction. If someone is rude, do not fight back. Block them and move on.

Report & Support

Tell a trusted adult and support the victim. A kind message can save someone’s day.
