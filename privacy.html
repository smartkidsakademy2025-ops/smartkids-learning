Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartKids Learning. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our students, parents, and teachers. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our educational tools, typing drills, and coding lessons.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized learning experience, we may collect the following information during registration:

  • Student Profile: Name, age, class level, and school name.
  • Account Details: Username, school email, and location (State/Country).
  • Performance Data: Scores from CBT quizzes and typing speed results.

3. How We Use Information

The data collected is used solely for:

  • Creating student accounts and saving learning progress.
  • Generating quiz results for teachers and parents to review.
  • Improving our educational content and user experience.
  • Ensuring a safe and secure environment for children.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We do not sell, rent, or trade student data with third parties for marketing purposes. We encourage parents and teachers to supervise children's online activities. We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary for the educational use of the platform.

5. Google AdSense & Cookies

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including IndexedDB for offline storage and secure encryption, to protect your data from unauthorized access.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your child's data, please contact us at:
privacy@smartkidslearning.org
