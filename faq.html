Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about our coding classes, digital literacy programs, and how we support young creators.

Kidventor Academy is a learning platform that teaches children coding, keyboard/mouse skills, digital literacy, and moral values. We focus on turning children from tech users into tech creators.
We support children from ages 6 to 18. Our lessons are tiered—beginners start with mouse control and typing, while older students dive into website development and logic.

Do you offer CBT Exam Prep?

Yes! We specifically train students in keyboard speed, mouse accuracy, and the confidence needed to navigate Computer Based Testing environments effectively.
