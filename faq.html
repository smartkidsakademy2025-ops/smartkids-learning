What is Kidventor Academy?

Kidventor Academy is a digital learning platform designed specifically for children. We combine technical skills like coding and web design with essential life lessons through our Moral Stories.

Is the content safe for my child?

Safety is our top priority. Our platform is a "walled garden," meaning all content is pre-vetted. We don't have open chat rooms, and our AI lessons focus on safe, responsible use of technology.

How do "Stars" and "XP" work?

We use gamification to keep kids engaged. When a student completes a mission or reads a moral story, they earn Stars. These Stars reflect their progress and can be used to unlock new "Quest" ranks on their Adventure page.

Do I need to pay for any courses?

Currently, Kidventor Academy is free to use. We support our mission through curated advertisements. This allows us to provide high-quality tech education to future leaders regardless of their background.
