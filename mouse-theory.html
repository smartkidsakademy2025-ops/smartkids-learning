Digital Handshake

The Left Button

Think of the Left Click as your "Select" finger. We use the **Index Finger** to tap it once to pick something or twice to open a game.

Secret Options

The Right Button

The Right Click is like a "Secret Menu." Use your **Middle Finger** to see hidden options like Copy, Paste, or Rename.

Travel Through Pages

The Scroll Wheel

The little tire in the middle helps you move up and down long pages without clicking. It's perfect for reading long books or news.

↖️
Precision

Cursor Control

The arrow follows your hand. Move smoothly and gently to aim at small buttons or icons. Precision is key for Computer Based Tests (CBT)!

📦
Move Items

Drag and Drop

Click and hold, move your hand, then let go. It's like picking up a toy and moving it to a new shelf. You can move files this way!
