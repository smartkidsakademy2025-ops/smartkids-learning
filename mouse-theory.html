Lesson 1 — Mouse Parts

A computer mouse has important parts that help you control the screen.

  • • Left Button → Select and open
  • • Right Button → Secret options menu
  • • Scroll Wheel → Move up and down
  • • Pointer → The arrow on screen
  • • Sensor → Helps the mouse detect movement
  • • Cable or Wireless Receiver → Connects the mouse

Lesson 5 — Drag and Drop

Drag and Drop means clicking and holding an item, moving it somewhere else, then releasing it.

This helps you move files, images, folders, and objects inside the computer.
