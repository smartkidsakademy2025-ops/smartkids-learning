Digital Literacy 💻

Digital literacy means knowing how to use computers, phones, the internet, and technology safely, wisely, and confidently in everyday life.

🖥️

1. What is Digital Literacy?

It is the ability to use digital devices like computers, tablets, phones, and the internet to learn, work, communicate, and solve problems.

2. Using the Internet

The internet helps us search for information, attend online classes, send emails, watch lessons, and communicate with people around the world.

3. Staying Safe Online

Never share passwords, personal details, or private information with strangers. Always ask a trusted adult before clicking suspicious links.

4. Communication Skills

Digital literacy includes writing emails, sending messages politely, joining online meetings, and using digital tools respectfully.

5. Learning with Technology

6. Responsible Use

Good digital citizens respect others online, avoid cyberbullying, give credit for work, and use technology for positive purposes.

Final Summary

Digital literacy is not just about using a computer — it is about thinking, learning, creating, and staying safe in the digital world.
