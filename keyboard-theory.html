Feel the tiny bumps on the F and J keys! That's where your "index knights" rest.

The middle row of keys is the Home Row. Your fingers always start here! Imagine they are in their beds, ready to jump up or down to hit other keys.
Use your Thumbs for the Space Bar. Use the Pinky for the Shift Key when you want to make a letter big (Capital) or use a symbol!
The Enter Key is the "Go" button. Use it to send messages, finish a line, or start a new paragraph in your digital stories.
