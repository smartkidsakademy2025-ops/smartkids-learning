What is a Toolbar?

Most professional apps (Word, PowerPoint, Excel) use toolbars. Learning to move your mouse precisely between the workspace and the buttons is a vital speed-skill.

Year 5 Objective

  • ✅ Switch between 4 different colors.
  • ✅ Draw a square and a circle.
  • ✅ Successfully find and click the "Clear" button.
