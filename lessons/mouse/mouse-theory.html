Mouse Anatomy

🖱️
⬅️ Left Click (Action!)
Right Click (Menu) ➡️
⬇️ Scroll Wheel (Elevator)
The left button is your primary tool. Think of it as your index finger pointing at something and saying "I want that!" You use it for 90% of your work.
The right button opens the "Secret Menu" (Context Menu). It shows you hidden options like Copy, Paste, or Delete based on where you click!
The wheel in the middle is like an elevator. Roll it forward to go up, and backward to go down. Great for long stories or big websites!
