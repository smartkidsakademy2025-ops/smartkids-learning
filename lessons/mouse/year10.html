Digital Literacy Concept: The Firewall

A Firewall is like a digital security guard for your computer. It monitors the "traffic" (information) coming in and out. By using Toggles and Sliders, administrators can decide how strict the security should be. In Year 10, we learn that security is a balance between protection and accessibility.
