The Year 12 Concept: What is an LLM?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI systems trained on massive amounts of text. They don't "think" like humans, but they use Probability to predict the next word in a sentence. By mastering Prompt Engineering (giving clear instructions), you can use AI to solve complex problems, write code, or learn new languages like never before.
