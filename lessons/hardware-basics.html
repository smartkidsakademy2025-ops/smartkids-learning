Module 1: Hardware

Computer Hardware

If you can touch it, it's hardware! Let's explore the physical parts of a computer.

Meet the "Big Four"

Most desktop computers are made up of these four main components.

🖥️

The Monitor

The "face" of the computer. It displays images, text, and videos so you can see what you are doing.

🔌

The System Unit (CPU)

The "brain" box. It contains the electronic parts that do all the hard work and thinking.

⌨️

The Keyboard

Your main way to "talk" to the computer using letters, numbers, and symbols.

🖱️

The Mouse

A pointing device that lets you select items on the screen and move things around.

➕ What are Peripherals?

Peripherals are extra parts we plug into the computer to give it more "superpowers." Common examples include Printers (to put work on paper), Speakers (to hear sound), and Webcams (to see your friends).
